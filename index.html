FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it free?

Yes. Manan is free and always will be.

Is it safe?

All messages between Manan users are end-to-end encrypted.

Is it Screenshot-Proof?

Screenshots have the potential of making the impermanent permanent. However with Manan you are protected.

What happens to the messages?

Messages disappear forever after they are read once, making them as private and secure as the spoken word.

What advantages does Manan have?

Encryption, Authentication, Deniability and Perfect forward secrecy.

What is OTR?

Off-the-Record Messaging (OTR) is a cryptographic protocol that provides encryption for instant messaging conversations.

What is Strophe.js?

Strophe.js is an XMPP library for JavaScript. Its primary purpose is to enable web-based, real-time XMPP applications that run in any browser.

How to use Manan?

Facebook login is supported for easy use.
